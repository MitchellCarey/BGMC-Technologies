Who we are

BGMC Technologies Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. We help businesses improve websites, products, automations, dashboards, internal tools and AI-assisted workflows.

For privacy matters, contact: hello@bgmc-technologies.co.uk.

What personal data we collect

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company or organisation name
  • Job title or role, if provided
  • Enquiry details
  • Website, product, workflow or process information submitted for review
  • Files, links, screenshots or technical details voluntarily sent to us
  • Communications between you and us
  • Basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited and server logs
  • Analytics data from Cloudflare Web Analytics or similar analytics tools, if used

How we collect personal data

We collect personal data when:

  • You email us
  • You contact us through the website
  • You request a review, quote or proposal
  • You send us information about a website, product, workflow or process
  • You interact with our social media profiles
  • You visit the website and technical logs or analytics are generated

Why we use personal data

We use personal data to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide the free "3-Point Fix List" or similar review
  • Understand your business, website, product or process
  • Prepare proposals, quotes or recommendations
  • Deliver services to clients
  • Manage client relationships
  • Maintain records
  • Improve our website and services
  • Keep the website secure
  • Comply with legal, tax and accounting obligations

Lawful basis

Depending on the situation, we rely on:

  • Legitimate interests: to respond to business enquiries, review submitted materials, improve our services, keep records and secure the website.
  • Contract or pre-contract steps: where we are preparing a proposal, quote or delivering services.
  • Legal obligation: where we need to keep records for tax, accounting or compliance reasons.
  • Consent: where consent is specifically requested, such as for optional marketing or certain non-essential cookies.

Sharing personal data

We may share personal data with trusted service providers where necessary, such as:

  • Website hosting and security providers
  • Email providers
  • Website form-processing providers
  • Analytics providers, including Cloudflare Web Analytics where enabled
  • CRM or project-management tools
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Professional advisers such as accountants or lawyers
  • AI, automation or software tools used to review or process submitted materials, where relevant to the service

We do not sell personal data.

International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the UK. Where this happens, we will take appropriate steps to protect personal data, such as using providers with suitable contractual safeguards.

How long we keep personal data

We keep personal data only for as long as needed. As a guide:

  • General enquiries: up to 24 months after the last interaction.
  • Client and project records: for the duration of the relationship and then as needed for legal, tax, accounting or dispute purposes.
  • Website logs: for a limited period needed for security and maintenance.
  • Marketing data: until consent is withdrawn or the person unsubscribes.

Your rights

Individuals may have rights to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Restrict processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact: hello@bgmc-technologies.co.uk.

Complaints

If you have a privacy concern, please contact us first at hello@bgmc-technologies.co.uk.

You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office.

Cookies and similar technologies

The website may use essential technical tools needed for hosting, security and performance.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors use the website and improve it. Cloudflare Web Analytics is intended to provide aggregate website analytics without advertising cookies or retargeting trackers.

We do not currently use advertising pixels or retargeting trackers. If we add non-essential cookies or similar technologies that require consent, we will ask for consent before setting them.

The website may link to social media platforms. Those platforms may use their own cookies or tracking technologies when you visit them.
